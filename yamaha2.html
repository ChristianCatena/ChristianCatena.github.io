Every advanced technological component of the R1 has been developed based on the knowledge Yamaha has gained from competing at the highest levels. The extraordinary Euro 5 crossplane 4-cylinder 998cc engine is descended directly from the M1, while the aerodynamic chassis is inspired by track designs. But it's the R1's incredible intelligent electronics that make this bike so special. Equipped with every conceivable element, from ride-by-wire throttle to Launch Control System (LCS), Engine Brake Management (EBM), Brake Control (BC), and much more, the R1 is the ultimate Yamaha Supersport, built to dominate both road and track. The model is available in the new sporty bicolour Icon Blue scheme, with matte blue coloring, as well as in the dynamic Yamaha Black option.
